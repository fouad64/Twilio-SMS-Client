Twilio Credentials
Error

The SMS verification code could not be sent to your phone. This is usually caused by incorrect or missing Twilio credentials entered during registration.

1
Invalid Account SID
Your Twilio Account SID must start with AC and be exactly 34 characters. Find it on your Twilio Console dashboard.
2
Wrong Auth Token
The Auth Token you provided does not match the one in your Twilio Console, or it may have been regenerated. Copy it directly from the console.
3
Invalid Sender Phone Number
The Allowed Sender ID must be a phone number you purchased and own in your Twilio account. Format: +1234567890
